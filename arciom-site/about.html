About Arciom

AI is making knowledge free. It's making judgment scarce, and scarce things are valuable.

Our mission

Every new AI model release pushes the price of commodity knowledge toward zero. What doesn't commoditize is the judgment of people who've done the work long enough to know whether a spike is auction pressure or ad fatigue, to spot the exceptions to the rules, and to make the calls that don't show up in any playbook.

Right now, that judgment isn't captured anywhere. It walks out the door when someone retires, changes jobs, or gets promoted, and the organization starts over.

Arciom exists to change that: to capture real expertise, prove it against the best available AI, and make sure the people who created it keep sharing in the value it produces, not just once but for as long as it keeps working.

The name

Arciom joins two old words.

An axiom is a truth so proven you can build on it, which is exactly what a captured decision becomes once it's beaten the best AI and held up. An arch is how individual pieces become a structure that outlasts its builders: each stone bearing weight the others make possible, held together by a keystone.

In every twin, your expert is the keystone.

Arciom: proven truths, built to last.
